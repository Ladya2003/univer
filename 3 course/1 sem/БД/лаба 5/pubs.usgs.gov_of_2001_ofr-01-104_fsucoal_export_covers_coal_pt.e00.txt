  Description:


    Abstract:


      This shapefile contains points that describe coal locations with


      associated coal quality data in the countries comprising the


      Former Soviet Union. (Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Estonia,


      Georgia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Latvia, Lithuania, Moldova,


      Russia, Tajikstan, Turkmenistan, Ukraine and Uzbekistan.)


      This spatial dataset is available as an ArcView shapefile,


      an Arc/Info export file, an Arc/Info ascii ungenerate file


      and as Spatial Data Transfer Standard (SDTS) files.


    Purpose:


      This spatial dataset is intended to delineate coal locations with


      associated coal quality data and to link to coal quality data in


      U.S.G.S. Open File Report 01-104, "Coal Quality and Resources


      of the Former Soviet Union - An ArcView Project".
